How ChargeShare Works

For EV Drivers

1
Create an account with Google, verify your email and phone.
2
Search for chargers near you by location, connector type, and availability.
3
Book instantly or send a booking request to the host.
4
Get the address only after your booking is confirmed or approved.
5
Charge, check out, and leave a review for the host.

For Charger Hosts

1
Sign up and verify your identity and charger.
2
List your charger with photos, connector info, schedule, and pricing.
3
Accept bookings automatically or manually approve each request.
4
Earn income from every charging session. Payments are held securely.
5
Enable/disable your listing at any time with one tap.
